Common Questions

We recommend booking at least 6–12 months in advance, especially for peak season travel (July–October and January–February). However, we can sometimes accommodate shorter lead times depending on availability. The earlier you get in touch, the more options we have for lodges and camps.

Absolutely! While we specialise in private safaris for couples and small families, we regularly organise trips for groups of up to 12 or more. Multiple vehicles and guides can be arranged to ensure every member of your party has an exceptional experience.

We offer safaris across a range of budgets. From comfortable mid-range lodges to ultra-luxury tented camps, we tailor every journey to match your expectations and budget. Share your budget range with us and we'll design the best possible experience within it.

Our safaris typically include all accommodation, meals, park entrance fees, private vehicle with an experienced English/French-speaking guide, all transfers, and domestic flights where applicable. International flights and travel insurance are generally not included, but we're happy to advise. We'll provide a detailed breakdown with every proposal.

Tanzania is one of Africa's most stable and welcoming countries for tourists. Safari tourism is well-established, and the national parks are professionally managed. As local experts based in Arusha, we ensure every aspect of your trip is safe and well-organised. We also provide comprehensive pre-travel advice on health, vaccinations, and travel tips.

Most nationalities require a visa to enter Tanzania, which can now be obtained online through the Tanzania eVisa portal before you travel. We'll provide you with all the details and guidance you need to make the process straightforward. Processing typically takes 2–10 business days.
