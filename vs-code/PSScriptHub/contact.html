Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our PowerShell scripts and services.

How do I request a custom script?

Simply fill out the contact form above with "Script Help" as the category and describe your automation needs. We'll work with you to create a tailored solution.

Can I contribute my own scripts?

Absolutely! We welcome community contributions. Select "Script Contribution" in the contact form and include your script details or GitHub repository link.

Do you provide enterprise support?

Yes, we offer enterprise-level PowerShell automation consulting and custom script development. Contact us to discuss your organization's needs.

How often are scripts updated?

Our script library is continuously updated with new additions and improvements. Critical updates are released as needed, with regular feature updates monthly.

Is there a cost for using scripts?

All scripts in our public library are free to use. Premium enterprise features and custom development services are available for a fee.

What PowerShell versions are supported?

Our scripts support PowerShell 5.1 and PowerShell 7+ (Core). Compatibility information is clearly marked on each script page.
