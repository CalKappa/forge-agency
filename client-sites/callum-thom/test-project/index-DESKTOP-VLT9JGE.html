The Perfect Grilled Chicken Recipe

This simple grilled chicken recipe delivers restaurant-quality results every time. The secret is in the preparation - properly seasoned chicken cooked at the right temperature for maximum juiciness.

Ingredients

15 Prep Time (min)
25 Cook Time (min)
4 Servings
  • 4 chicken breasts (6-8 oz each)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried thyme
Target Temperature: Internal temperature should reach 165°F (74°C)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat (375-400°F). Clean and oil the grates to prevent sticking.
  2. Pat chicken breasts dry with paper towels. Using a meat mallet, pound to even thickness (about 3/4 inch).
  3. Mix salt, pepper, garlic powder, paprika, onion powder, and thyme in a small bowl. Rub chicken with olive oil, then coat evenly with seasoning mix.
  4. Grill chicken for 6-7 minutes per side without moving. Check internal temperature with a meat thermometer - it should reach 165°F.
  5. Remove from grill and let chicken rest for 5 minutes before slicing. This allows juices to redistribute for maximum tenderness.
Pro Tip: Let chicken come to room temperature for 15 minutes before grilling for even cooking.
